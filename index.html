SafeStart is patient engagement surgical safety solution that eliminates

Never Events and reduce delay in operating room

Created by a surgeon with a passion for safety

SafeStart uses mobile, cloud-based, technology compliant with HIPPA to create an enhanced pre-surgical patient care record complete with surgeon annotated photos, allergies, medications, x-rays, and consent forms.



Multiple repeat reviews and sign-offs by all clinical staff, the patient, and facility assure an accurate correct record to allow for procedures to start safely and on time.

5,000+

Never Events Happen per year in us operating rooms.

40%

Of never events originate in the office.

Avialable on desktop and mobile

  • Eliminate Never Events
  • Increase Patient Engagement
  • Reduce Delay and Cancellations

Never events are unacceptable, avoidable, and cause great harm to patients as well as impacting doctors nurses and their hospitals

When used correctly SafeStart eliminates wrong patient, wrong procedure, wrong site never events.

Engaged patients lead to improved health outcomes, better patient care, and lower costs.

SafeStart's design includes the patient and family as part of the care team that includes the facility and anesthesia service.

Reduce Delays, Cancellations, and No Shows that disrupt patient flow, increase operational costs, raise patient anxiety and cause revenue loss.

All stakeholders vet SafeStart's comprehensive graphically rich patient care document set multiple times before the patient arrives at the preoperative area and in the preoperative area streamlining a safe care process so procedures can start on time.
